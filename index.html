What is Tuka?

Tuka is a digital music platform for sharing and marketing music content.
Artists can sell their songs to curators and fans so they can share it with their network.
Fans can create profiles to search and filter content snippets by genre keywords
Curators can share purchased content with their networks via snippets and receive token rewards.
Copyright is secured through blockchain records of all data transactions. Users own and control their data for additional compensation.
